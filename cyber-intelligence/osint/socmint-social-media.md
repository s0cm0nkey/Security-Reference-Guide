<summary>General SOCMINT Techniques and Best Practices</summary>

* **Operational Security**
  * Use dedicated OSINT accounts separate from personal profiles
  * Consider using VPNs or Tor for sensitive investigations
  * Be aware of platform terms of service and legal boundaries
  * Document your methodology for potential legal proceedings
* **Verification Techniques**
  * Cross-reference information across multiple platforms
  * Use reverse image search to verify profile pictures (Google Images, TinEye, Yandex)
  * Check metadata in downloaded images and videos
  * Verify timestamps and geolocation data
* **Collection Best Practices**
  * Take timestamped screenshots of all evidence
  * Archive pages immediately using archive.today or Wayback Machine
  * Export data in multiple formats when possible
  * Maintain detailed chain of custody documentation
* **Analysis Frameworks**
  * Use timeline analysis tools to correlate activity across platforms
  * Map social networks and connections between accounts
  * Identify patterns in posting behavior and content
  * Look for account linking through shared usernames, emails, or phone numbers
* **Legal and Ethical Considerations**
  * Understand jurisdiction-specific privacy laws (GDPR, CCPA, etc.)
  * Respect platform terms of service
  * Avoid social engineering or impersonation
  * Consider ethical implications of investigation methods
* **Useful Resources**
  * [OSINT Framework](https://osintframework.com/) - Comprehensive directory of OSINT tools
  * [IntelTechniques](https://inteltechniques.com/tools/) - Collection of custom search tools
  * [Bellingcat's Online Investigation Toolkit](https://bit.ly/bcattools) - Curated OSINT resource list

</details>
